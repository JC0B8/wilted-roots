Where the living world meets the human hand

Eight artists drawn from varying backgrounds and mediums—earth, creatures, breath, and touch—proposing that “nature” and “culture” are already tangled in the same root system.

Curated by the OpenThinkingFreely initiative · Free and open to all

Statement

This exhibition gathers practices that refuse a clean split between wild and built, ecology and body, soil and story. It asks how we recognize ourselves as part of—not masters over—shared habitats.
